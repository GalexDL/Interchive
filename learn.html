What is the Interchive

Interchive is a term I came up with meaning online archive.
The Interchive is an application that attempts to replicate previous dragalia stories or to create new ones! This is no where near completion and neither are all of the tools. Huge credits to @ChaoSpam for some of the menu designs/buttons/animations, Also thank you to some of the few people who helped me learn to pave and learn renpy, I really appreciate the help so far!
